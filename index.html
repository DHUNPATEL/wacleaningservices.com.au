FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cleaning services in Perth.

Do you offer a bond back guarantee? +

Yes — we offer a 100% bond back guarantee on all end-of-lease cleans. If your property manager or landlord is not satisfied with the result, we return and re-clean the property at absolutely no extra cost to you. We stand behind our work completely.

What areas in Perth do you service? +

We service all suburbs across Greater Perth — from Joondalup and Wanneroo in the north, to Rockingham and Baldivis in the south, Midland and Swan Valley in the east, and Fremantle in the west. Not sure if we cover your suburb? Call us on 0483 818 854 and we'll confirm instantly.

How long does an end-of-lease clean take? +

It depends on the size and condition of the property. A standard 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom home typically takes 4–8 hours. Larger properties or those requiring extra attention may take longer. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when you get your quote.

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies and equipment? +

Absolutely. Our team arrives fully equipped with all professional-grade, eco-friendly cleaning products, vacuums, steam cleaners and specialised tools. You don't need to provide anything — just let us in and we'll handle the rest.

Can I get a same-day booking? +

Yes! We offer same-day and next-day bookings across Perth at no extra charge, subject to availability. Call us on 0483 818 854 for urgent bookings, or submit our online quote form and select your preferred date — we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Are your cleaners insured and police-checked? +

Yes. Every cleaner on our team is police-checked, background-verified and covered under our full public liability insurance policy. Your property and belongings are safe in our hands, and you're covered in the unlikely event of any incident.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfer and cash. Payment is generally due on the day of the clean. We'll confirm the preferred payment method when confirming your booking.

Do I need to be home during the clean? +

No — you don't need to be present. Many clients simply leave a key or provide access instructions. Our fully vetted and insured team will lock up securely when finished. You'll receive a completion notification once the job is done.
