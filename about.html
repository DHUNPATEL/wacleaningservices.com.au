Our Story

Local, Trusted & Built on Results

WA Cleaning Services was founded right here in Perth by people who understood a simple truth: when you're moving out of a rental, the last thing you need is a cleaner who cuts corners and costs you your bond.

We started with a single focus — end-of-lease cleaning done so thoroughly that no property manager could ever find fault. That commitment to detail, transparency and guaranteed results quickly earned us a reputation as Perth's go-to cleaning specialists.

Today we serve 500+ happy clients across every corner of Greater Perth, offering everything from regular home cleans to commercial and carpet cleaning — but our bond-back guarantee remains the standard we hold every single job to.
